It is used in Tustin Transform.\newline Hence, we can replace the s by the above term in discrete time system, so: \[ \frac{v_{ok} - v_{o(k-1)}}{\Delta _T} + av_o(t)= \frac{v_{ik} - v_{i(k-1)}}{\Delta _T} + bv_i(t) \] The output voltage is can be written as the function of $v_{ik}$, $v_{i(k-1)}$ and $v_{o(k-1)}$. And this is recursive. This provides a great advantage of the modern computers.
