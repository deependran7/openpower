Matlab is a scientific computational enviornment used by scientists, engineers, mathamaticians and programmers to model system, anaysing the system dynamics, developing algorithms and so on. In power engineering it plays a crucial role to model and understand the system. Since, power engineering deals with the systems having no direct visual observations, direct exprimental analysis can be very tedious. What the matlab it provies an enviromnent where we can obesrve the electrical phenomena in form of data and ghaphical visualisation.

It is one of the part of scientific computation where the problems is broken down into small parts and a suitable algoritm is applied in each and the final result is combined to observe results. But matlab provies very short hand operators and funtions which simplifies the whole algorithm, which were supposed to be used in classical moddeling, in to simple one.

Here, we will focus in both procedural as well as graphical approach of problem solving in matlab. In procedural approache we write fucntions and codes to obtain results while in graphical approcach we drag and drop the funchtional blocks in a algorithmic sequence and run them to obtain the results. Both of the approaches has own importances and consequences. Here, we focus on modelling the electrical system and obesrving their response on different situation mathematically.

Here MATLAB and SIMULINK are introduced you with different projects. This will help to learn the software as well as develop the skill to develop projects in MATLAB and SIMULINK. You will learn their use through examples and their explanations and analysis.
