Response of RLC Circuit

The general diagram of series RLC circuit is shown below:

The theoretical concepts of respose of RLC can be studied either online or using reference books. Here, we will focus on the modelling the circuit and obtaining the response of circuit in different inputs and different circuit conditons.

Open a new simulink model file and drag and drop the necessary block from the simulink, simscape library. They are Series RLC branch, dc voltage source, ac voltage source, scope, powergui, current measurement and ground.
And connect them as follows:


After conncting them set the values of R, L and C values.
a.Step Reponse:
Here you put a dc voltage source in input and all other component in series. There are generally to case of response. They are
Underdamping case
The value of R, L and C for this case are $10 \Omega, 1 mH , 1 \mu Farad$.

Overdamped Case
The values of R, L and C for this case are $100 \Omega, 1mH , 1\mu Farad$



b. AC response and resonance
For general ac resoponse you can pur the ac source at the source and run the simulation. You can observe the result of 50 hz frequecy.
